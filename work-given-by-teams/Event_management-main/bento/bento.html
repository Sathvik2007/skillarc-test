Participation Guide

  1. 1
    Register Online

    Complete registration before the deadline. Submit your student ID and department. Confirmation arrives by email within 24 hours.

  2. 2
    Attend & Mark Attendance

    Biometric attendance is recorded at the entry gate. A minimum of 75% presence is required across all sessions.

  3. 3
    Participate Actively

    Engage in Q&A, visit lab stations, and optionally submit a research poster. Best poster and best question awards at valediction.

  4. 4
    Receive Your Certificate

    E-certificates emailed within 3 working days. Physical certificates distributed at closing ceremony to in-person attendees.
